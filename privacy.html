Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate Arcyn Studios:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, games played, and interaction events collected via standard web analytics.
  • Device data: Browser type, screen resolution, and general device information for optimization.
  • Local storage: Favorites, settings, and high scores stored locally on your device only.
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for theme preference and functionality. No advertising cookies.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to improve platform performance, personalize your experience, and maintain game statistics. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies

Arcyn Studios uses essential cookies for theme preferences and functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect data. Local storage data never leaves your device. No account system currently exists, minimizing data exposure.

5. Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents should supervise minor usage.

6. Your Rights

You may clear local data at any time via browser settings. Since we don't maintain user accounts, there is no profile data to delete. Contact us for any privacy concerns.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the platform evolves. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance. Check this page periodically for updates.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, reach out via our contact form.
